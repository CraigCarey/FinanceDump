    About VGOLD-CORE

    VGOLD- CORE is a pure gold price tracking token backed by 3.8 million ounces of JORC and NI 43-101 certified in-situ gold reserves. Unlike other gold tokens that track physical bars in vaults, VGOLD-CORE represents beneficial interest in professionally managed, independently verified gold reserves held by VGOLD - SP .

    VGOLD Global Note Program

    In addition to the VGOLD-CORE token, VGOLD-SP offers secured Global Notes backed by the same JORC/NI 43-101 certified gold reserves. These institutional-grade debt instruments provide fixed income exposure with the security of first-priority collateral over gold assets. Notes feature competitive interest rates (fixed or floating), tenors from 1–5 years, and an optional physical gold settlement at maturity. Maximum 50 investors per series, minimum investment $1,000,000. The Global Note Program enables institutional investors to participate in gold-backed yield opportunities while mining partners access working capital against proven reserves.
