    Executive Summary

    QGP SPC is a regulated Cayman Islands–based segregated portfolio company purpose-built for the institutional tokenization of real-world assets (RWAs). It serves as the issuance and structuring vehicle for VGOLD-CORE and the VGOLD Global Note Program, providing legal clarity, asset segregation, and investor protection.

    The platform enables institutions to hold, trade, and finance physical assets—including gold, resources, and receivables—in digital form without compromising on legal certainty, auditability, or regulatory compliance.

    The QGP Approach: Structured for Institutions

    Segregated Portfolios

    Each asset class or issuance resides in its own legally siloed portfolio within the SPC. No cross-contamination of risk. Every portfolio is independently accounted, audited, and insulated.

    Legal Certainty

    Assets are held via perfected security interests, deeds of assignment, and tier-1 custody arrangements—not merely “wrapped” or represented by unsecured promises. Legal title is clear and enforceable.

    Regulatory Alignment

    Designed to operate within existing securities, commodities, and banking frameworks. The structure supports Basel III treatment of gold as a high-quality liquid asset (HQLA) where applicable, with legal opinions confirming non-virtual asset, non-security status under applicable law.

    QGP in Practice: Powering VGOLD-CORE

    QGP SPC acts as the issuer of VGOLD-CORE tokens, each representing beneficial interest in 3.8 million ounces of JORC and NI 43-101 certified in-situ gold reserves.

    The platform also manages the VGOLD Global Note Program, offering secured, gold-backed debt instruments with fixed income characteristics—tenors from 1–5 years, competitive interest rates, and optional physical gold settlement at maturity.

    The structure is intentionally clean: maximum 50 investors globally, no public offering, restricted transferability, and full KYC/AML compliance. This preserves exempt status and ensures suitability for institutional balance sheets.

    What QGP Is Not

    Addressing the scrutiny that surrounds digital asset structures.

    Not a "crypto project"

    No unregulated DeFi, no anonymous trading, no unaudited reserves. Every portfolio is professionally administered with independent oversight.

    Not a stablecoin or unsecured token

    Fully backed by independently verified, legally assigned physical assets with monthly attestations and bi-annual audits.

    Not retail-facing

    Purely institutional. All investors undergo KYC/AML verification and execute subscription agreements.

    Not an unregistered security

    Structured under exemptions available to qualified institutional buyers (QIBs) and accredited investors, supported by legal opinions.
